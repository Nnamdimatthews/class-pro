Sonic Advance

Sonic Advance is a 2001 platform game developed by Dimps and published by Sega for the Game Boy Advance. It is an installment in the Sonic the Hedgehog series and the first game in the Sonic Advance series. The story follows Sonic as he sets out to stop Dr. Eggman from taking over the world using his new invention, the Egg Mobile. Gameplay consists of the player completing various levels as one of four characters, each with their own unique attributes. After each zone is completed, the player faces Dr. Eggman in a boss battle.

In Sonic Advance, the player can play as all the main characters, including Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, and Amy. The game features a unique gameplay mechanic where the player can switch between characters at any time. The game also features a multiplayer mode where up to four players can play together.

Also in Sonic Advance, there are 9 levels and 9 bosses. Sonic Advance consists of nine Zones, most of which are split into two Acts. Any Act can be played or replayed from the "Select a Zone" screen. After the last Act, the player faces a boss in a separate Act. The Zones in their order are: 1.Neo Green Hill 2.Secret Base 3.Casino Paradise 4.Ice Mountain 5.Angel Island 6.Egg Rocket 7.Cosmic Angel 8.X-Zone 9.The Moon Zone The bosses are in order: 1. EggHammerTank 2. Egg Press 3. Egg Ball 4. Egg Spider 5. Mecha Knuckles 6. Egg Snake 7. Egg Mobile-H 8. Egg Mobile-D 9. Super Egg Robot

Now you might be wondering "Why Does The moon zone not have a cool name like the other zones?" Well, That's because the zone is named after the moon, which is a celestial body and not a place with a specific name. And also it's another extra zone in the game, so of course sonic has to travel to the moon and stop Eggman from taking over the world.

In the final battle, Sonic has to face the Super Egg Robot, which is a giant robot created by Dr. Eggman. To defeat him, Sonic must use all the chaos emeralds to turn super.

After defeating the Super Egg Robot, Sonic saves the day and stops Dr. Eggman's evil plans. The game ends with Sonic and his friends celebrating their victory and looking forward to their next adventure.
