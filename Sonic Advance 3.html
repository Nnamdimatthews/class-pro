Sonic Advance 3

Sonic Advance 3 is a 2004 platform game developed by Sonic Team and Dimps and published by Sega for the Game Boy Advance. It is part of the Sonic the Hedgehog series, and the final sequel to Sonic Advance 2. The game stars the characters Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Amy, and Cream as they seek to keep Doctor Eggman and his robot assistant Gemerl from building empires on each of seven chunks Eggman has divided the Earth into.

Sonic Advance 3 is the last game in the Sonic Advance series, and the last game in the series to be released on the Game Boy Advance. Sonic Advance 3 was released on November 18, 2004 in Japan, December 1, 2004 in North America, and December 2, 2004 in Europe

In sonic advance 3, the player can play as all the main characters, including Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Amy, and Cream. The game features a unique gameplay mechanic where the player can switch between characters at any time. The game also features a multiplayer mode where up to four players can play together.

Also in sonic advance 3, there are 9 levels and 9 bosses in total (The Bosses are in brackets).
